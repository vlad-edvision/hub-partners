AI & Computer Science
Algorithms, data science, cybersecurity, large language models. Python programming (+ HTML/C++ depending on level). Hands-on mini-projects: building algorithms, working with datasets, simulating AI use cases. Ethics: bias, data privacy, automation impact.

Business & Finance
Strategic planning, ethical leadership, marketing, financial management, global operations. Students develop business plans, evaluate ethical dilemmas, create marketing campaigns, analyse global markets. Financial concepts: budgeting, forecasting, investment strategies.
Tutor: Mr Matthew Imison — economics educator and former finance professional with global experience across UK, Asia and Africa.
Programme Structure — 2-week residential
Subject Tutorials
24 hours total
Small-group, university-style sessions with expert tutors. Each student produces a final essay or project and receives written feedback and a tailored letter of recommendation.
Industry Project
12 hours total
Working in small teams, students tackle a real-world challenge connected to their chosen subject. Supported by industry partners. Concludes with group presentations and a Certificate of Completion.
Guest Speaker Sessions
3 hours total
Each week, students hear from a leading voice in academia, business, tech or medicine — offering big-picture thinking, inspiration and a glimpse into real-world impact.
